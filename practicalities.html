Transport

Train

Trains go from Oslo to Krisiansand or Stavanger to Kristiansand. Book your train tickets early (the same one-way ticket may vary in price dependent on availability from 199 NKR up to 600 NKR).


Flights

There are direct flights to Kjevik Airport from all major cities in Norway and from Amsterdam, Copenhagen and Gdansk. To get to the city centre from the airport there is a local bus service that runs once an hour. Look for bus no 35 departing from platform B just outside the domestic arrival hall.


Ferry

Color Line sails from Hirtshals (Denmark) to Kristiansand twice a day.
