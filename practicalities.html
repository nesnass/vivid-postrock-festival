Venue

We are happy to announce that the festival will take place in the old cinema in town, with its massive backdrop for visuals and central location, and concrete 80ies architecture. You will find Aladdin, senter for film, litteratur, musikk og scenekunst in Kongensgate 6


This year we are serving homemade turkish food and local brews!

Transport

Train

Trains go from Oslo to Krisiansand or Stavanger to Kristiansand. Book your train tickets early (the same one-way ticket may vary in price dependent on availability from 199 NKR up to 600 NKR).


Flights

There are direct flights to Kjevik Airport from all mojor cities in Norway and from Amsterdam, Copenhagen, Gdansk and London Stansted. To get to the city centre from the airport there is a local bus service that runs once an hour (bus no 35) and a flybuss which are running just outside the domestic arrival hall. To see the timeschedule, please see here. A typical route from airport to city centre would be Kjevik Lufthavn -> Markens Gate.


Ferry

Color Line sail from Hirtshals (Denmark) to Kristiansand twice a day.
