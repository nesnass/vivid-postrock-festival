VISUAL ARTISTS 2020

Jo Ravn Abusland // Drongo

Abusland graduated from the Art Academy in Bergen and the Kunsthochschule Berlin-Weissensee. Abusland works in a wide range of different materials and techniques, with themes related to identity and difference.


Ingrid Bjørnaali // Nordsind

Ingrid Kristensen Bjørnaali (b. 1991) is studying MFA at the Oslo National Academy of the Arts and recently went on exchange at the The University of Arts Helsinki. She mainly works with video art and VJing. In her video practice, she captures virtual and physical landscapes via various camera technologies and softwares, exploring how people might relate to and interact with places, non-places, physical objects and their digital representations.

Alex Dickson // Glacier

Having worked closely with various bands and musicians across Europe and beyond; Alex delivers striking visual work that often depicts natural scenes that are twisted and contorted many times over; almost to the point at which they become indistinguishable from their original form. He achieves this through a combination of repetition and duplication and also via an amalgamation of analogue and digital signal processing that allows for new visual forms to take hold. His previous work includes collaborations with bands such as Kokomo, Watered and Sleepmakeswaves and electronic artists Gagarin, Nolan Dialta and Daniel Adam.

Sascha Anton // Kokomo

Sascha Anton is an Art director from the Ruhr area. He currently lives in Berlin. Driven by ideas and conceptual thinking, He does art direction, editorial design, film and motion design.

Amenra // Amenra

The venue is an old junior highschool. Unused, where we rehearse for almost 15 years now. We have a couple of classrooms to call ours. I love the desolate nature of the place. Its where we recorded MASS III for instance, filmed a lot of videos and visuals.


Renata Portelada & Håvard Tveito // All Shall Be Well, Liongeist

They are artists and architects who work between the physical and the digital. They have previously worked in London and Sao Paulo and are today based in Kristiansand.
