VISUAL ARTISTS 2019

417.3 [RU]

We always used nature videos at our shows and only recently decided to collaborate with the VJ, who in real time creates video abstractions on the screen. His name is Mikael Bayrikyan. He performed at Mujuice, Philip Gorbachev, Nikita Zabelin and many other shows, and also created videos for the project Fountain. In addition, Mikael plays audiovisual live under the nickname Synchronome (aka BSNVT) and managed to show up at the last Error_404 (techno festival), as well as at the international festival of electronic music "Electro Mechanica".

At the VIVID festival we will present a video in which abstractions and nature will be combined.

A Swarm of the Sun [SE]

A Swarm of the Sun has always treated the visual part of their albums with the same dignity as the music itself. It's also something they've consistently created themselves. Jakob Berglund has art directed all albums, as well as produced video, photography and designed and developed for web and digital platforms. So it should come as no surprise that the band keeps this tight creative control when they bring their vision to the stage. Jakob seeks to create abstract and intense imagery - where uncanny and raw emotion is hidden in the cracks of stillness. It's an expansion of "The Woods" - the band's latest album - but also something new.

At VIVID, A Swarm of the Sun is teaming up with graphical designer and VJ Peter Wallentin, who will mix and manipulate Jakob's visuals throughout the set. He is currently based in Stockholm, but has a been an active VJ in the Malmö club scene.
